About This Ministry

This ministry is committed to the clear teaching of Scripture according to the principle of right division. Our emphasis is the revelation of the Mystery given to the Apostle Paul and the distinctiveness of the Body of Christ.

Statement of Faith

We believe in the verbal inspiration of the Scriptures, the deity of the Lord Jesus Christ, salvation by grace through faith apart from works, and the distinct apostleship of Paul as the apostle of the Gentiles in this present dispensation of grace.

We recognize the distinction between Israel and the Body of Christ and seek to interpret Scripture in its proper dispensational setting.
